Our Story

Growing up in the 2000’s was an interesting time especially for people who enjoyed music. I grew up in Dandora that put me in contact with a lot of musical influences from different diverse people. My mother  introduced me to Souls and classic  likes of Madonna Michael Jackson, Bobby McFerrin ,Celine Dion, Nina Simon , to name but a few. There used to be this barber shop outside our flat had this really cool Eminem poster. I asked him who he was and he played me some Eminem cassettes and I was an instant shady fun then my older cousins who used to baby sit me introduced me to shows that  showed Kenyan music Mizizi. This show paved way for other shows like the beat st8up and the likes this is how I used to enjoy my music unless the occasional time when “the cool cousin brought home their CD mixes”  and we blasted Tupac, Biggie the Beasty boyz Wu tang clan Mob Deep  Nazizi Ukoo Flani Mau Mau,  Kalamashaka, Jua Kali. This love for music kept me sane it was my escape when things got tough living in the environment I did   I was lucky I found music. And now ad like to give back
